A. DJI Flagship Store (大疆深圳歡樂海岸旗艦店)

Shenzhen DJI Technology Co., Ltd. (深圳大疆創新科技有限公司), commonly known as DJI (大疆), is a Chinese technology company specializing in the production and development of civilian drones (無人機), aerial photography equipment （航拍照片器材）, optical imaging systems, and automotive components.

DJI Flagship Store is located in Nanshan District (南山區), which is an area known for its technological development. The DJI flagship store focuses on immersive experiences for visitors, showcasing the company's full range of consumer products and hosting interactive offline events. Visitors can experience cutting-edge drone and imaging technology, watch live flight demonstrations, and view stunning aerial footage (航拍視頻).

DJI drones lead the industry in wireless technology applications, establishing the company as a global leader in both commercial and consumer aerial imaging and drone innovation. Starting with commercial autonomous flight control systems, DJI has expanded its product line to include flight controllers, drones, and aerial cameras. These products are widely used in film production, agriculture, construction, search and rescue, and environmental conservation. As of October 2020, DJI holds over 80% of the global market share in civilian and commercial drones (民用和商業無人機), ranking first among commercial drone manufacturers worldwide.
