Traditional Papermaking Research and Study Base 古法造紙研學基地

Papermaking (造紙術) is one of China's Four Great Inventions (中國古代的四大發明) and also part of the nation's significant intangible cultural heritage. As the first village of traditional papermaking (中國古法造紙第一村), Fuli Village (扶利村) in Zhaoqing has been passing down its traditional papermaking techniques for over eight hundred years since the Southern Song Dynasty till now. This craft has also been included in the Guangdong Provincial Intangible Cultural Heritage List.

With the rise of the "intangible cultural heritage + tourism" (非遺+旅遊) and "intangible cultural heritage + cultural creativity" (非遺+文創) modes, the traditional papermaking research and study base was established in 2024 in the Fuli village to help people of different ages to recognize and understand this intangible cultural heritage craft. Through the four processes including exploration, study, practice, and reflection, participants can explore the entire handmade papermaking process through firsthand experience, from selecting materials to producing finished products, fostering a deeper appreciation for papermaking history and cultural heritage.
