Foshan 佛山

Foshan is a key city in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area (GBA) (粵港澳大灣區), known as the "9+2" region ("9+2" 區域). Foshan is a blend of tradition and modernity, making it an intriguing destination for visitors and a key player in China's economic landscape.

Foshan is recognized for its rich cultural heritage, including martial arts (武術) and traditional crafts (傳統工藝).
