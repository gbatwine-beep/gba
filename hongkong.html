Hong Kong 香港

Hong Kong, a cosmopolitan metropolis nestled along the southern coast of China, is renowned for its unique blend of eastern and western cultures. As the world's third-largest financial center and a vital international trading port, Hong Kong leverages the dual advantages of the "One Country, Two Systems" principle to play a pivotal role in the development of the Greater Bay Area (GBA). It actively promotes and supports regional economic growth, enhances the GBA's function in national development, and facilitates the expansion of Hong Kong's competitive industries within the region—leveraging its strengths to serve the nation's needs.

Hong Kong boasts many iconic landmarks. Its dazzling nightscape is world-famous, earning recognition as one of the "World's Three Great Night Views" by Japan's Night View Tourism Convention Bureau.
