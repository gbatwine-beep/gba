Zhuhai 珠海

Zhuhai is a vibrant coastal city located in Guangdong Province, and it is well-known for its beautiful scenery and rich culture. It is also an important economic hub (經濟樞紐), focusing on industries such as technology and tourism.

Zhuhai was named the most livable city in China (中國最宜居城市) by the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences in 2019. Zhuhai is also effectively integrating technology and multiplying 5G applications in a "smart-city approach" (智慧城市方法) in different service industries, including education and tourism, so as to enhance the efficiency and sustainability (效率和可持續性) in the city.

According to the city's 14th Five-Year Plan (十四五規劃), Zhuhai will continue to develop into a global advanced manufacturing base (全球先進製造業基地) and a centre for industrial innovation (產業創新). Zhuhai is a dynamic city that combines natural beauty, economic growth, and cultural richness.

In addition, the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge (港珠澳大橋) has brought Hong Kong and Zhuhai significantly closer together, creating favorable conditions for Hong Kong's participation in the development of Zhuhai.
