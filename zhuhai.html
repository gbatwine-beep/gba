Zhuhai 珠海

Zhuhai is a vibrant coastal city located in Guangdong Province, and it is well-known for its beautiful scenery and rich culture. It is also an important economic hub, focusing on industries such as technology and tourism.

Zhuhai was named the most livable city in China by the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences in 2019. Zhuhai is also effectively integrating technology and multiplying 5G applications in a "smart-city approach" in different service industries, including education and tourism, so as to enhance the efficiency and sustainability in the city.

According to the city's 14th Five-Year Plan, Zhuhai will continue to develop into a global advanced manufacturing base and a centre for industrial innovation. Zhuhai is a dynamic city that combines natural beauty, economic growth, and cultural richness.

In addition, the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge has brought Hong Kong and Zhuhai significantly closer together, creating favorable conditions for Hong Kong's participation in the development of Zhuhai.
