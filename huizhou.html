Huizhou 惠州

Huizhou is situated in the east coast of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA) (粵港澳大灣區). It is known as the "Famous County in Lingnan" (「嶺南名郡」) and has the second largest area among the Mainland cities in the Greater Bay Area. With the rich tourism resources and a beautiful ecological environment, Huizhou was rated as one of the first national ecological civilization demonstration cities (首批國家生態文明建設示範市).

Huizhou has a resident population of approximately 6.066 million, making it the sixth largest city in the Greater Bay Area, just behind Hong Kong. Additionally, Huizhou is renowned as a significant Hakka (客家) emigrant hometown in Guangdong Province and stands as one of the most important settlements for the Hakka people.
