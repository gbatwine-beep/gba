Luofu Mountain Scenic Area (羅浮山景區)

Mount Luofu is one of the ten famous Taoist mountains in China (十大道教名山之一). It is awarded as a national 5A-rated tourist attraction (國家5A級旅遊景區).

The main peak of Mount Luofu is Feiyunding (飛雲頂), which is 1,296 meters above sea level. It is a national key scenic spot and summer resort in China, and is known as the "Foremost Peak of Lingnan" (「嶺南第一山」).

Mount Luofu is characterized by three main features: a) unique peaks and rocks (奇峰怪石), b) beautiful waterfalls and springs (飛瀑名泉), and c) intriguing caves and wonders (洞天奇景). Each zone offers distinct experiences for visitors and highlights the natural beauty of the area. The majestic mountains and caves create a mystical landscape that has attracted numerous poets and philosophers for centuries. Renowned literary figures such as Li Bai (李白), Du Fu (杜甫), Han Yu (韓愈), and Su Shi (蘇軾) have composed verses that beautifully capture the mountain's sublime beauty.
