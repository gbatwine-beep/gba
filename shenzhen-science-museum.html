B. Shenzhen Science and Technology Museum (深圳科學技術館)

The Shenzhen Science and Technology Museum is designed around the theme "Digital Civilization, Innovative Future" (「數字文明、創新未來」). It aims to become the world's first digital civilization-themed science museum and a benchmark for science museums nationwide. From its futuristic architecture to its advanced exhibition technologies and interactive experiences, the museum stands as a leader in scientific innovation.

The Shenzhen Science and Technology Museum hosts approximately 950 exhibits that cover a wide range of scientific disciplines and technological innovations. These exhibits highlight significant advancements in fields such as robotics, artificial intelligence, telecommunications, and environmental technology. Many of the displays focus on homegrown innovations, showcasing the achievements of Chinese scientists and engineers.

There is an AI-powered robotic restaurant that provides fully automated dining (全自動餐飲) in the museum. Special sections are often dedicated to themes like smart cities, sustainable development, and the future of transportation, reflecting Shenzhen's status as a leading tech hub.

Interactive installations in the museum allow visitors to engage with the technology, making complex concepts accessible and enjoyable. The museum not only aims to educate the public but also to inspire future generations of innovators and thinkers.
