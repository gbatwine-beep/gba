Double-Moon Bay (雙月灣)

Double-Moon Bay (雙月灣) is renowned for its stunning natural beauty. It is surrounded by the majestic Daqing Mountain (大青山) and faces Daya Bay (大亞灣) and Honghai Bay (紅海灣), forming two crescent-shaped bays (半月形海灣).

Double-Moon Bay spans roughly 1.5 kilometers in length, with a width ranging from 200 to 300 meters. From a bird's eye view, it looks like two crescents leaning against each other, leading to its poetic name - 'Double-Moon Bay.'

Double-Moon Bay is renowned for its rich and diverse marine life, making it a paradise for nature enthusiasts and water sports lovers. The waters of the bay are crystal clear. The blue ocean forms a sharp contrast with the white sandy beach. With the gentle ocean current, the bay is perfect for a variety of water activities such as swimming, diving, and surfing.

The bay features vibrant coral reefs (珊瑚礁) that provide habitats for a variety of colorful tropical fish species such as clownfish (小丑魚) and angelfish (天使魚). The abundance of marine life makes Double-Moon Bay a fantastic destination for eco-tourism and underwater exploration.
