Jiangmen 江門

Jiangmen is located in the Guangdong province of southern China, situated in the western part of the Pearl River Delta. It has been integrated into the "one-hour economic circle" of the Greater Pearl River Delta, which highlights its strategic connectivity and economic collaboration with nearby major cities, such as Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Hong Kong. This integration is further enhanced by high-speed rail services, which have significantly reduced travel times, making Jiangmen increasingly favorable for businesses and tourists.

Jiangmen is actively promoting sustainability initiatives to preserve its natural and cultural resources. Preservation of cultural heritage sites is prioritized so as to maintain the community's historical identity while promoting tourism. Efforts have also been made to develop green spaces, such as parks and gardens. These spaces aim not only to beautify the urban landscape but also serve as habitats for local wildlife, creating a paradise for birds and other species.
