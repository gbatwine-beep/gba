Shenzhen 深圳

Shenzhen is a key player in the global technology landscape. As a special economic zone (經濟特區) and a national innovative city (國家創新型城市), it is recognized for its technological achievements, including wireless technology (無線技術), information and communication technology (ICT) (信息通信技術), and artificial intelligence (AI).

Shenzhen is particularly noted for its advancements in 5G technology. It has also achieved significant advancements in electric vehicles (電動汽車), transitioning its public transport to electric and enhancing smart manufacturing (智能製造) through automation and robotics to enhance production efficiency.

Shenzhen is a dynamic technology hub renowned for its rapid growth and innovation. The Shenzhen Innovation and Technology Bureau (深圳市科技創新委員會) actively promotes research and development by offering incentives to both startups and established companies. Entrepreneurs are attracted to the city due to its abundant access to capital, talent, and resources.
