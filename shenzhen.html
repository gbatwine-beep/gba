Shenzhen 深圳

Shenzhen is a key player in the global technology landscape. As a special economic zone and a national innovative city, it is recognized for its technological achievements, including wireless technology, information and communication technology (ICT), and artificial intelligence (AI).

Shenzhen is particularly noted for its advancements in 5G technology. It has also achieved significant advancements in electric vehicles, transitioning its public transport to electric and enhancing smart manufacturing through automation and robotics to enhance production efficiency.

Shenzhen is a dynamic technology hub renowned for its rapid growth and innovation. The Shenzhen Innovation and Technology Bureau actively promotes research and development by offering incentives to both startups and established companies. Entrepreneurs are attracted to the city due to its abundant access to capital, talent, and resources.
