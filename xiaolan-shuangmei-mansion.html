Xiaolan Shuangmei Mansion (小欖雙美府)

Xiaolan Shuangmei Mansion (小欖雙美府) was recognized as a historical building of Zhongshan and designated as a Provincial Cultural Heritage Site (省級文物保護單位) in 2019. Shuangmei Mansion exemplifies the fusion of traditional Lingnan (Cantonese) architecture (廣府傳統民居) and Nanyang (Southeast Asian) colonial (南洋殖民) influences, making it a masterpiece of overseas Chinese architecture (華僑建築) in Zhongshan.

The mansion's primary wooden framework (主體架構) utilizes traditional Lingnan mortise-and-tenon (榫卯) joinery, which secures the structure without the use of nails or metal fasteners, demonstrating the ingenuity of Chinese timber architecture.

The complex harmoniously blends blue-brick houses (青磚房屋) with curved "wok ear" gables (鑊耳山牆) and stained-glass European windows (彩色玻璃歐式窗戶), while its courtyards feature traditional Cantonese sliding lattice doors (趟櫳門) alongside Roman-style fountains (羅馬噴泉).

The site contributes to the preservation of the local culture and history that Dr. Sun Yat-sen (孫中山) emphasized in his vision for a modern China, highlighting the importance of cultural identity.
