Zhaoqing (肇庆)

Zhaoqing (肇庆) is one of the key cities situated within the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA) (粵港澳大灣區), located in the central-western part of Guangdong Province. Additionally, Zhaoqing is a national historical and cultural city, known as the "Inkstone Capital of China" (中國硯都).

Zhaoqing is distinguished by its rich historical and cultural heritage, complemented by its stunning landscapes. The city has a long history of urban development and is equally committed to preserving and promoting its intangible cultural heritage (非物質文化遺產). This dedication ensures that Zhaoqing not only showcases its beautiful scenery but also honors and advances its cultural traditions.
