China International Aviation & Aerospace Exhibition (中國國際航空航太博覽會)

China International Aviation & Aerospace Exhibition is also known as Airshow China, which is the largest aviation exhibition in mainland China. This Airshow has been held every 2 years since 1996 in Zhuhai, supporting the growth of the aviation industry, such as aircraft, private flying, and air tourism.

The airshow features a wide range of global displays about innovative aviation and aerospace technology. Civi aircraft C919, China's first domestically developed large passenger aircraft, has been showcased at the airshow. It highlights China's advancements in aerospace technology, attracting significant attention from industry professionals and aviation enthusiasts. Rapid advancements in drone technology have also been demonstrated at the airshow in Zhuhai, and it allows participants to get a real experience in drone operation.

The China International Aviation & Aerospace Exhibition serves as a significant platform for promoting international exchange and economic cooperation. A remarkable increase in foreign participation was recorded in Airshow China 2024, which attracted over 40 countries and regions joining the event.
