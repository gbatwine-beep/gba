B. Keyuan Museum 可園博物館

Keyuan (可園) is one of the Four classical gardens of Guangdong Province (廣東四大名園) from China's Qing Dynasty, originally constructed in 1850.

Keyuan operates as the Keyuan Museum and is one of the National Key Cultural Heritage Sites (全國重點文物保護單位) nowadays. In 2025, it was ranked fourth among Guangdong's most innovative museums, highlighting its ability to showcase creativity while preserving tradition.

Keyuan Museum incorporated AI elements and Augmented Reality (AR) technology. The interactive experiences, such as educational games and digital reconstruction of architectural features, emphasize the concept of using technology to integrate old and new elements in Keyuan Museum, allowing visitors to witness the collision of traditional architectural design and modern technology.

Keyuan Museum stands as a testament to how technology can enrich our understanding and appreciation of cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination for those interested in the interplay of history and innovation.
