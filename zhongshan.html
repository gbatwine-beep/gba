Zhongshan 中山

Zhongshan is located on the west bank of the Pearl River Estuary, with a superior geographical location. It is an important node in the central and southern Pearl River Delta. It plays an important role as a transportation hub and manufacturing base in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA).

Zhongshan City is the only city in China named after a modern leader, highlighting Sun Yat-sen's (孫中山) significance to the region. As his birthplace, the city features numerous landmarks that celebrate his legacy.
