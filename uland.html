A. ULAND 悠蘭里

ULAND is located in the Songshanhu (松山湖) scenic area in Dongguan and is a new cultural and artistic landmark that is related to STEAM education. As a destination that integrates technology, culture, and art, Zone A in ULAND features five major exhibition halls. Two examples that relate to these elements are the Mathematics Exploration Hall and the Chinese Root Art Museum.

The Mathematics Exploration Hall (數學探索館) is the first mathematical-themed science museum located in China. Through various interactive devices, participants can stimulate their curiosity and creativity through the concept of "learning by doing" (做中學) and understand different mathematical concepts, which is also one of the ideas in STEAM education.

The Chinese Root Art Museum (中國根書藝術館) in ULAND is dedicated to showcasing the rich heritage of Chinese calligraphy and art. Root art calligraphy (根書) combines root carving (根雕) with calligraphy by using tree roots and imagination to create Chinese characters. It emphasizes the cultural significance of traditional Chinese roots, blending historical artistry with contemporary expressions.

The artistic works by root calligraphy artist Mr. Yang Yubing in the Chinese Root Art Museum allow visitors to intuitively experience the evolution of Chinese characters through this traditional carving art format. Through various exhibitions and educational programs, visitors gain a deeper appreciation for these Chinese traditions and their ongoing relevance in today's society, fostering a connection between the past and present.
