Guangzhou 廣州

Guangzhou is one of China's first-tier cities. Its GDP exceeded $2.8 trillion RMB in 2022, ranking 2nd among the 11 cities in the Greater Bay Area (right behind Shenzhen).

The transport system is very well-developed in Guangzhou. The first metro line in Guangzhou was built in 1997. Currently, the metro system in Guangzhou operates 18 lines. The total operating mileage of its metro reaches 621 km, ranking 1st in the Greater Bay Area.
