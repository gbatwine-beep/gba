Zhuhai Grand Theatre 珠海大劇院

The Zhuhai Grand Theatre is a prominent cultural landmark located in Zhuhai, playing a significant role in promoting the arts. It was opened in 2015 and is situated near the coast.

The design of the Zhuhai Grand Theatre is very distinctive. As the architecture comprises one large and one small "seashell", it is popularly nicknamed "The Seashell of Sun and Moon" (日月貝). The concept of the shell-shaped structures was inspired by Zhuhai's coastal features, offering beautiful views for enhancing the overall experience for visitors.

The Zhuhai Grand Theatre serves as a cultural hub for the region, promoting the arts and hosting international and local performances. The theatre is mainly divided into two zones, including a large auditorium that can seat over 1,600 people, designed for local and international performances such as concerts, operas, and drama shows (e.g. Phantom of the Opera, 宮崎駿久石讓印象集音樂會, etc).
