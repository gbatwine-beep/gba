B. Pearl River Night Cruise 珠江夜游

The Pearl River night cruise in Guangzhou is a popular activity that offers a unique way to experience Guangzhou's stunning skyline and iconic atmosphere. The Pearl River is the third-longest river in China and the largest in Southern China.

The local government and investors recognized the tourism potential of the river in the 1980s after the economic reforms, and the Pearl River night cruises started to be a formalized tourist attraction in Guangzhou.

There are different cruise options with various durations. The cruises typically last around one hour, providing a panoramic view of the bustling riverfront, showcasing the contrast between modern architecture and traditional buildings, such as the Canton Tower (廣州塔), Guangzhou Opera House (廣州大劇院), Liede Bridge (獵德大橋), and Zhujiang New Town (珠江新城).

On a Pearl River night cruise in Guangzhou, passengers may experience traditional music and dance performances that showcase local culture and heritage. Some cruises offer commentary about the landmarks and history of Guangzhou, enhancing a better understanding of the city.
