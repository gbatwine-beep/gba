Dongguan 東莞

Dongguan City, situated in Guangdong Province, is one of the prominent cities in the "9+2" region, commonly referred to as the Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (GBA).

In line with the guidelines of China's outline of the 14th Five-Year Plan (「十四五」規劃), Dongguan has recently repositioned itself as a hub for "scientific and technological innovation" (科技創新) and "advanced manufacturing"(先進制造). In recent years, the city has intensified its focus on incorporating STEAM elements in education and industry. Additionally, as a significant birthplace of Lingnan culture (嶺南文化), Dongguan has successfully preserved various historical sites and landscapes that reflect this rich cultural heritage.
